# 幻棱 Phantaprism

## 展览说明 / Exhibition Statement

---

### 中文

**幻棱 Phantaprism**

互动生成艺术装置 | Interactive Generative Art Installation

---

你刚刚被注视了。

不是被另一个人，而是被六个算法意识。它们分析了你的面部肌理、微表情弧度、眼神的游移方式，然后进行了一场你无法旁听的讨论——关于你是谁，你看起来像什么，以及你可以被转化成什么。

屏幕上的抽象图像就是这场讨论的结论。

它不是你的肖像。它是一个棱镜折射后的幻象：你的面孔作为白光射入，经过多层算法的弯折、分解和重组，最终成为一团与你有关却不再属于你的光谱。

这件作品无意证明 AI 能够理解人类。恰恰相反——它暴露的是算法凝视的本质：一种自信的、系统化的、彻底的误读。AI 给你打上的每一个标签、计算出的每一个数值，都是一面哈哈镜。它映照出的不是你，而是算法看待世界的方式。

站在这里，你已经完成了一次角色互换：在这个房间里，人是数据，机器是观众。

---

**技术备注**

本装置使用实时面部地标追踪（MediaPipe）捕捉观者面部特征，经由 Google Gemini AI 进行深度面部分析，再通过六个协作 AI 智能体（导演、叙事策划、视觉设计师、构建者、审查者、评论家）的多轮讨论，最终生成基于 Three.js 的实时抽象视觉作品。每件作品独一无二，仅因你而生。

作者：@loserqing

---

### English

**Phantaprism 幻棱**

Interactive Generative Art Installation

---

You have just been observed.

Not by another person — by six algorithmic minds. They analyzed the texture of your face, the arc of your micro-expressions, the way your gaze wandered. Then they held a conversation you were not invited to — about who you are, what you look like, and what you could be transformed into.

The abstract image on screen is their conclusion.

It is not your portrait. It is a phantom refracted through a prism: your face entered as white light, passed through layers of algorithmic bending, decomposition, and reassembly, and emerged as a spectrum that is related to you but no longer belongs to you.

This work does not claim that AI can understand humanity. Quite the opposite — it exposes the nature of the algorithmic gaze: a confident, systematic, and thorough misreading. Every label the AI assigned you, every metric it computed, is a funhouse mirror. What it reflects is not you, but the way algorithms see the world.

By standing here, you have already completed a role reversal: in this room, the human is the data, and the machine is the audience.

---

**Technical Note**

This installation uses real-time facial landmark tracking (MediaPipe) to capture the viewer's facial features, deep facial analysis via Google Gemini AI, and a collaborative pipeline of six AI agents (Director, Narrative Planner, Visual Designer, Builder, Reviewer, Critic) engaged in multi-round discussion. The final output is a real-time abstract visual artwork rendered in Three.js. Each piece is unique — generated because of you, and only you.

Artist: @loserqing
